Our Story

Crafting Excellence Since 2005

What started as a small family-run engraving business in a modest workshop has grown into one of the region's most trusted custom awards specialists — and we're proud of every step of the journey.

Founded by master engraver Robert Harrison, our shop has always held the same philosophy: every award tells a story, and we are privileged to help tell it. Over 50,000 awards later, that passion hasn't dimmed for a single day.

We serve everyone from primary school sports days to Fortune 500 corporate galas, bringing the same dedication and craftsmanship to every single piece, regardless of quantity or budget.

Our Journey

2005

The Beginning

Robert Harrison opens a small engraving shop with a single rotary machine, serving the local community.

2009

First Laser System

Invested in our first industrial laser engraving system, expanding capabilities to crystal and glass awards.

2013

Corporate Expansion

Established dedicated corporate accounts program serving regional businesses, banks, and law firms.

2017

Online Store Launch

Launched our online ordering platform, bringing our services to customers nationwide.

2022

3D Laser Technology

Added 3D subsurface laser engraving — creating stunning three-dimensional artwork inside crystal.

Still Growing
