Premium Material Options

Each material has unique properties suited to different award types and occasions.

Premium
Crystal

Optical crystal glass delivers brilliance and clarity unmatched by any other material. Ideal for high-end corporate and executive awards.

Glass

High-quality flat and beveled glass awards offer a modern, clean aesthetic. Excellent for desktop plaques and wall-mounted recognition pieces.

Laser Engravable Modern Look Color Fill
Acrylic

Lightweight, shatter-resistant acrylic in clear, frosted, and tinted options. Vibrant full-color printing and precision laser engraving compatible.

Full Color Print Laser Engravable Budget Friendly Multiple Colors
Metal

Brass, aluminum, and stainless steel options for a bold, authoritative look. Deep rotary or laser engraving creates lasting impressions.

Rotary Engraving Laser Marking Brass / Aluminum Durable
Wood

Rich natural woods including walnut, cherry, and maple. Laser engraving on wood produces a warm, high-contrast result perfect for executive plaques.

Laser Engravable Walnut / Cherry Natural Grain Classic Look

High-quality cast resin allows for complex trophy shapes in vibrant colors. Ideal for sports trophies and novelty award designs at affordable price points.
